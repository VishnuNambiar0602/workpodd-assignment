# E-Commerce Store Refund Policy

## Section 1: General Refund Conditions and Limits
- **Refund Policy Window**: All refund requests must be submitted within the specific window defined for each product category. The window starts from the date of delivery.
- **Annual Refund Cap**: To prevent abuse, each customer profile is subject to a strict annual refund cap of $200. Any refund that would cause the customer's total refund amount in the current calendar year to exceed $200 must be escalated to a human administrator for review, regardless of policy compliance.
- **Tier-based Restocking Fee**: Standard tier customers are subject to a 10% restocking fee for physical items unless the item is verified as defective. Premium tier customers are exempt from all restocking fees.

## Section 2: Electronics Category Rules
- **Electronics Refund Window**: Electronics items are eligible for refund requests within 15 days of the delivery date.
- **Condition Requirements**: The item must be returned in its original packaging with all original accessories, manuals, and documentation.
- **Defect Verification**: For electronics refund requests submitted more than 7 days after delivery, the customer must provide proof of defect (such as a photo, diagnostic report, or error code). Without proof, the refund must be denied.

## Section 3: Apparel Category Rules
- **Apparel Refund Window**: Apparel items are eligible for refund requests within 30 days of the delivery date.
- **Condition Requirements**: Apparel must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags and labels attached.
- **Exclusion (Final Sale)**: Any apparel items purchased with a "FINAL-SALE" tag, or purchased during clearance events, are strictly non-refundable and cannot be returned under any circumstances.

## Section 4: Digital Goods Category Rules
- **Digital Goods Non-Refundable**: All digital purchases (e.g., software licenses, e-books, virtual currency, downloadable content) are non-refundable once downloaded, activated, or accessed.
- **Defect Exception**: If a customer reports a technical defect (e.g., license key fails to activate, download file is corrupted), a refund can be approved within 7 days of the purchase date, provided the customer support representative can verify the license status.

## Section 5: Customer Behavior and Abuse Prevention
- **High Refund Frequency**: If a customer has a `past_refund_count` of 3 or more in their history, their request must be immediately escalated to a human administrator. The automated agent is not authorized to approve refunds for high-frequency refund accounts.
- **Holiday Sale Restocking Fee**: Any items purchased during major holiday sale events (e.g., Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Prime Week) are subject to a 50% restocking fee for standard customers and a 20% restocking fee for premium customers, unless defective.
